Traffic Management System

  • The Traffic Management System (TMS) is a digital platform designed to enhance road safety and improve the efficiency of traffic operations. It enables real-time monitoring, reporting, and management of traffic violations within designated areas, particularly accident-prone zones.
  • The system includes two main user roles: Administrator and Traffic Enforcer. The Administrator oversees the entire system—managing enforcer accounts, assigning locations, reviewing reports, and maintaining system data integrity. Meanwhile, Traffic Enforcers are assigned to specific traffic lights or areas, where they can submit reports on traffic violations, accidents, and congestion directly through the system.
  • With its structured interface, the TMS promotes faster communication, accurate documentation, and improved decision-making for traffic authorities. By digitizing traffic enforcement and monitoring, the system helps ensure safer roads, more organized traffic flow, and more transparent reporting processes.
